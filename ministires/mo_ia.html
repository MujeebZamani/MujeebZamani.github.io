Ministry Of Interior Affairs

Interior Security & Public Administration

ABOUT THIS MINISTRY
The Ministry of Interior Affairs is responsible for managing the nation’s domestic administration, including civil registration, public safety, community governance, and Interior security coordination. We work to ensure efficient public service delivery and maintain stability within the country.
